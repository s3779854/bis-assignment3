Project Background

Motivation

Video games have been around for decades and become a mature industry. Many of our group members are interested in game development. However, the limitations of our knowledge and technologies prevent us from making a large scale video game. So, we chose to focus on the game-genre of 2D platformer to pay homage to the classic Super Mario Bro. With about 12 weeks development, we decided to complete a 2D platformer game using our own sprite design, level design and sound design.

In addition, the current situation of COVID-19 outbreak motivated our group to design the related game theme. The name of our game is Coronavirus Counterattack. Hopefully, this game can provide some entertainment to people during the difficult time.

Aims and Goals

Aim

The aim of this project is using Unity game engine to develop a tiny 2D platformer game similar to Super Mario. Under the current situation of COVID-19 outbreak, our group wants to provide entertainment to people who need to stay at home to help them get through the difficult times. Therefore, making our game’s playability stronger is the key to success. To achieve this aim, we have planned to implement some features in the game as our goals.

Goals

Scope

As a group, we decided on a specific type of game and committed to that specific type of game design. The scope follows the features of a “Metroidvania” platformer, however due to that format having so many features, not all features were considered going into the final prototype. However, we chose essential features to implement. Many features that were not considered into the final prototype are touched on in the extended viable feature section.
