Skills and Jobs

Programmer:This position would involve game programming and ideally be fit for someone with Unity/C# experience but could also be fit for someone familiar with other game engines such as the Unreal Engine. In this position you will be programming features for the game and implementing them in the Unity engine, this would also include assisting other team members with more complicated implementation of features or assisting them with implementing an aspect of the project if they require any further assistance.

Game Designer:This position will involve designing the levels and gameplay of the game. For this position you will ideally have experience with game design as the design to games is fairly unique compared to other mediums. In this position you will be designing stages, gameplay mechanics and also contributing to the story, overall you will be guiding the direction of the game while meeting the goals of the original design specification.

Artist:This position will involve creating 2D art assets for the game. Ideally you will have experience with either pixel art or digital art and a portfolio to show us so we have an idea of whether or not your style will fit our needs. In this position you will also be acting as the Art Director so you can decide the overall art style including whether the game is pixel art or digital art.

Quality & Assurance Tester:This position will involve thoroughly testing the game as well as giving feedback on design aspects on the game. Ideally you will have experience testing software though a candidate with the right mindset would also fit in.
